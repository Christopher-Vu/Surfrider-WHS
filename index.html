The Surfrider Foundation

    The Surfrider Foundation is a 501 grassroots nonprofit. Founded in 1984 by a small group of surfers concerned about costal development in Malibu, California, The Surfrider Foundation has since grown into an interconnected national network of chapters dedicated to the protection of the world’s oceans, waves, and beaches.

    Surfrider Foundation hosts a myriad of campaigns devoted to the protection of all oceanic matters, from the mitigation of offshore drilling to sanctioning marine protected areas. The organization also leverages its vast network of chapters to bolster their campaigns as well as volunteer in their local communities.

How We Contribute

       Surfrider Foundation backs its dedication to these five pillars through advocacy, activism, and volunteer work. Surfrider Foundation WHS Club will be holding monthly/bi-monthly beach cleanups, as well as bi-monthly campus cleanups.

       Monthly general meetings will also be held to both update members on our upcoming service events and inform them on new Surfrider Foundation campaigns to partake in.
